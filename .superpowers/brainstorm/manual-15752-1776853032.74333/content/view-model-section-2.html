Revised design rule

Primary view = semantic scene canvas

The default working surface is a large world plane. Containers are shown spatially. Users reason visually first, not through a nested table.

Tree becomes support, not source

The tree or outline view is only for navigation, collapse/expand, bulk editing, and quick focus. It is not the primary mental model of the graph.

Containment and relation coexist

A node can appear inside a structural container visually, while also having relation edges to nodes in other containers, regions, or organizations.

Open implication

A

Container as visual zone

Organizations and regions are editable areas on the world plane. Dragging a person into a zone updates its structural membership.

B

Hybrid zone + inspector confirmation

Dragging changes temporary placement, but real membership is confirmed in the inspector to avoid ambiguous multi-membership.
